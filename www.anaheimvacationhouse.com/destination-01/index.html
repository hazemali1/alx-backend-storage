Destination #01

Stunning and highly upgraded pool home perfect for 3-4 families.  

Our six-bedroom home with four bathrooms is perfect for gathering the family in one location. There are three Cal-king master bedrooms, one queen, and two children's rooms with full over full bunk beds. One Cal-king master and one children's room have a shared hallway bathroom on the main floor. The queen bedroom and the other children's bedroom share an upstairs hallway bathroom. The remaining two Cal-king masters have en-suites and are located upstairs. All bedrooms have 43' LCD TVs.  House has hi-speed internet service..

A partially open-concept kitchen, family room, and dining area are perfect for enjoying each other's company. The upgraded kitchen has custom paneled cabinetry, granite countertops with full travertine backsplash, and stainless appliances: stove/oven, microwave, fridge, freezer, and dishwasher. The dining room has plenty of seating with two dining tables, and an oversized sectional couch has a queen sleeper sofa. 

Bathrooms are upgraded with travertine floors, granite countertops, and custom white antique and cherry wood inked cabinets. In addition, the walk-in showers are upgraded with custom-designed travertine and marble inlays and pattern designs. 

Scraped laminate wood flooring throughout the downstairs and upgraded carpet throughout the upstairs. The home has high-speed wireless internet. There is central heating and air. The garage has a full-size Billards table, a Stiga ping pong table, and a washer and dryer.

The rear yard has a beautiful in-ground pool & Jacuzzi with a child safety fence. There are child safety locks and alarms on all doors leading out to the pool to protect your children. There is outdoor dining located on our Flagstone patio with a cover. A natural gas bbq grill is situated off of the kitchen.  

The house is about a 3/4 mile walk to the Disneyland tram on Magic way. Watch Disney fireworks from the back/front yards. 

This house sleeps 15 in beds, and the Maximum Occupancy is 15 at any time
(this number is the combined total of overnight guests plus day visitors)
Maximum Vehicles 3. No RVs, Trailers, or Commercial Vehicles.
Minimum Stay 3 Nights
No Outside Music of Any Kind per Anaheim Code
No Outside noise between 10 pm and 9 am per city code. You must be inside during this time.
City of Anaheim House Permit/Business License# REG2014-00174

Pricing includes a mandatory pool/jacuzzi fee, pool heated to 82, and Jacuzzi to 102

All major credit/debit cards are accepted.
No cancellation refunds within 60 days of arrival. We recommend you purchase travel insurance.

All homes are professionally cleaned per CDC guidelines.

EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY
These houses are for overnight sleeping purposes ONLY. You CAN NOT rent these houses for ANY gathering or event. Homes are protected with outdoor security cameras. There are NO inside cameras.

All rentals booked within 2 weeks of arrival:
Extra screening/background checks and proof of payment are required.

We also reserve the right to cancel the reservation(s) of ANY booking with technical error(s) that includes but is not limited to; pricing, website difficulties, or any other issue that may arise in the booking stage of a reservation.
